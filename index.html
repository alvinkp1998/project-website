BIG 5 EUROPEAN LEAGUES

English Premier League, Italian Serie A, Spanish La Liga, German Bundesliga and French Ligue 1 are the top five ranked professional football leagues in the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) and have been recognized to be the most successful football leagues in the world. The success of ‘The Big Five’ leagues from a financial perspective has enabled teams to attract top-level players from all over the world
